Pasta Carbonara

Pasta Carbonara is a classic Italian dish.

It is a rich and creamy pasta dish perfect for any occasion.

It is incredibly flavorful.

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g Spaghetti or any pasta of your choice
  • 100g Pancetta or bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)

METHOD OF PREPARATION

  1. Preparing the Pasta

    1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
    2. Reserve some pasta water before draining the pasta.

  2. Preparing the Sauce

    1. Fry the pancetta or bacon in a pan over medium heat until crispy.
    2. Add the garlic clove for extra flavor, then remove it after a minute.
    3. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix with the grated Pecorino and Parmesan cheeses.
    4. Season with freshly ground black pepper.

  3. Final Touches

    1. Mix the cooked pasta with the crispy pancetta.
    2. Remove the pan from heat and quickly add the egg and cheese mixture, stirring constantly.
    3. Add a little pasta water if the sauce is too thick.
    4. Serve immediately, garnished with extra cheese and black pepper.
