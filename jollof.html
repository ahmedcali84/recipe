Jollof Rice

*Jollof Rice is a vibrant and flavorful West African dish.

* It features rice cooked in a spicy tomato sauce with a variety of vegetables and sometimes meat.

* It is often served at special occasions and gatherings.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (optional)

METHOD OF PREPARATION

  1. Preparing the Base

    1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
    2. Add the chopped onion and bell pepper, cooking until softened.
    3. Stir in the garlic and ginger, cooking for an additional minute.
    4. Add the tomato sauce, chicken broth, thyme, paprika, curry powder, and bay leaf. Stir well.

  2. Cooking the Rice

    1. Add the rice to the pot, stirring to coat with the tomato mixture.
    2. Add salt and pepper to taste.
    3. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.

  3. Final Touches

    1. Stir in the frozen peas and diced carrots, cooking for an additional 5 minutes.
    2. Add the cooked chicken if using, and heat through.
    3. Serve the Jollof Rice hot, and enjoy!
