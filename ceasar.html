Chicken Caesar Salad

*Chicken Caesar Salad is a popular salad dish.

* It combines tender chicken with crisp romaine lettuce and a tangy Caesar dressing.

* It is a hearty and satisfying meal.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)

METHOD OF PREPARATION

  1. Preparing the Chicken

    1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat a pan with a little olive oil over medium heat.
    3. Cook the chicken breasts for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
    4. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips.

  2. Assembling the Salad

    1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce with the Caesar dressing.
    2. Toss the lettuce to coat evenly with the dressing.
    3. Add the croutons and Parmesan cheese, and toss gently.

  3. Final Touches

    1. Top the salad with the sliced chicken.
    2. Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper if desired.
    3. Serve immediately and enjoy your Chicken Caesar Salad.
