Gulab Jamun

Information About the Dish

*Gulab jamun is an Indian dish.

* It is a delicacy served on special occasions or as a dessert.

* It is really tasty.

INGREDIENTS

  • Gulab Jamun Mix from the supermarket
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom (optional, for flavor)
  • Rose water (optional, for fragrance)

METHOD OF PREPARATION

  1. Gulab Jamun

    1. Put the mix in a bowl and add a quarter glass of water.
    2. Knead the mix into smooth dough.
    3. Prepare small balls from the dough.
    4. Put the small dough balls in a frying pan and fry them on medium heat.
    5. Fry the balls until golden brown.

  2. Sugar Syrup

    1. Put a cup of sugar in a pan and add a quarter glass of water.
    2. Heat them on medium heat.
    3. Add cardamom for scent and rose water if desired.
    4. Turn the heat off after the sugar has completely melted.

  3. Final Touches

    1. Put the fried gulab jamun in a bowl and add the sugar syrup.
    2. Let it soak for some time.
    3. Serve warm or chilled, and enjoy your sugar-soaked gulab jamun.
